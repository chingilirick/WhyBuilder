# WhyBuilder Language Guide

## Core Principles
- Direct, positive language
- No negative words (no, not, can't, won't)
- Trust-focused vocabulary
- Professional but warm tone

## Replacements

| Old (Negative) | New (Positive) |
|----------------|----------------|
| No properties found | Homes are waiting to be matched |
| No results | Let's refine your search |
| Failed to load | We're preparing your results |
| Error | We're looking into this |
| Something went wrong | We'll get this right |

## Trust Words
- Verified
- Trusted
- Reviewed
- Measured
- Confident
- Clear
- Transparent
- Secure

## Action Words
- Find your match
- Discover your home
- Choose with confidence
- Start your decision
- Get verified

## User-First Language
- Your home
- Your lifestyle
- Your decision
- Your match
- Your preferences
